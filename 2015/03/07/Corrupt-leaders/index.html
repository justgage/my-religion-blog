Corrupt leaders

Sat Mar 7 2015

There was a lot of different leaders in our world, many of which are corrupt. Here are some examples during Christ’s time.

Fig tree - Matthew 21:17-20

These where Jewish leaders that appeared healthy but actually where not producing any fruit.

The Second Son - Matthew 21:28-32

These where people that said the right things but their hearts where “far from me”. Those that say but don’t do.

The Husbandman - Matthew 21:33-41

These are those that killed the prophets.

The Wedding Guest - Matthew 22:1-14

These where people that where given the opportunity to get the gospel but didn’t take it serious.
